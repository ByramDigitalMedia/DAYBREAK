KNOW HIS NAME

Those who know your name trust in you, for you, Lord, have never forsaken those who seek you. ~Psalm 9:10

  • Those who know His name: God’s “name” is His character and nature. To know His name isn’t just intellectual—it means to personally recognize and experience who God is — His faithfulness, mercy, justice, and power.
  • Trust in Him: Because of that knowledge and experience, a Man naturally puts his confidence in God. Trust here implies reliance, security, and faith, even in uncertain times.
  • He has never forsaken those who seek Him: The testimony is from history and experience: God has consistently been faithful. Seeking God never results in abandonment; He does not turn His back on those who depend on Him.

God’s Love

Romans 5:8 – But God demonstrates his own love for us in this: While we were still sinners, Christ died for us.

This verse reveals the depth of God’s love—it is unconditional and sacrificial. Christ didn’t wait for us to become worthy or righteous; He gave His life while we were still lost in sin. It shows that God’s love is not based on our merit, but on His grace.
